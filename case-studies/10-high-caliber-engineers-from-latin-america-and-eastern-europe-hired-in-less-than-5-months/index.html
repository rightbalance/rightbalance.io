10+ high caliber engineers from Latin America and Eastern Europe hired in less than 5 months

Challenge

After raising $50M in a series B round, the engineering team was ready to scale rapidly to the next level with high caliber Fronted, Backend, DevOps and QA engineers from Latin America and Eastern Europe.

Solution

Right Balance augmented Emotives in-house team with lead-level engineers while keeping the bar high, hiring only senior self-starters to minimize the ramp-up time and being able to hit the ground running.

Right Balance helped us to scale the team. They have been a great partner for us, their communication and feedback allow us to quickly tune and identify what talent is needed and what caliber. Their pipeline has been amazing.

Oleg Pylnev, CTO @ Emotive (ex SpaceX, Honest Company, MySpace)

Execution

Right Balance accepted the challenge of sourcing only talented candidates from around the world in a short period of time to find the best and brightest engineers that meet Emotives high bar. By utilizing proprietary AI technology, a large network of talented engineers, and a world-class technical interview process, Right Balance team was able to present a constant stream of quality candidates, moving quickly to extend offers ahead of competitors, leading to closing all positions.

Results

10+ high-level engineers hired from Latin America and Eastern Europe having a large amount of experience in Django, React, Automated QA, and AWS DevOps. Many engineers have been promoted since the date of hire due to outstanding performance. Right Balance and Emotive continue having a strong and successful partnership, looking to keep hiring amazing and talented engineers during 2022 and beyond.
